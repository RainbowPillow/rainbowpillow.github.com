About Me

I am a final year Ph.D. candidate from Department of Ocean Operations and Civil Engineering, Norwegian University of Science and Technology (NTNU), Norway. I am a part of Mechatronics Lab, advised by Prof. Houxiang Zhang, Prof. Hans Petter Hildre, and Assoc. Prof. Guoyuan Li. Before coming to NTNU, I completed my Masters at Department of Computer Science, Zhejiang University of Technology, Zhejiang, China.

Research Interests

I am interested in Data Analytics for data-driven modeling of ship motion, Data-driven Sea State Estimation, Big Data, and Data Mining. My current research includes:

  • data-driven modeling of ship motion
    • Sensitivity analysis
    • Modeling for autonomous vessels
    • Modeling for ship motion and automatic docking
  • Data Driven Sea State Estimation
    • Time series classification
    • Transfer learning for sea state estimation
  • Digital twins for autonomous ships
  • Anomaly detection
